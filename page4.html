CEILING FAN
This lavish ceiling fan is a NEED in your new home! Powered by a powerbank, it is easily convinient and easy to use. It also comes witha light up LED so you can feel cool, and be able to see around your room at the same time.
The circuit of my LED is powered by the Pin 1 on my Microbit, and is connected to the ground too. I have one wire from the P1 to the circuit board at + and another for the ground connected to my board at -. This supplies power to my breadboard as whenever I need power, I just use a wire to connect it to the negative and positive strips on the side, without having to use another long wire to connect it to my microbit. Then I put together my circuit using a diode, a transistor, multiple wires, and a resistor. Here is a link to my Tinker Cad of this.
