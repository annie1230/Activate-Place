AUTOMATIC DOOR ALARM
Want a secure but fast way to enter your house without carrying around a key? We got your back! Trying to enter a house when it's dark outside and you can't find the switch to turn on the light, we made it easy for you, when you press the button to open your house it will automatically open the door and turn on the interior light. But what if someone tries to come in without pressing the key? The door will sound an alarm and flash the light o scare your intruder away!

HOW WAS THIS MADE?

This system was created by code, a microbit, a servo, a speaker and 2 breadboards. The mini breadboard was used on the outside to connect the mircobit and speaker to the breadboard on the indie which contains the LEDS and servo. The yellow LED is connected to PIN 2 and when the servo is connected to PIN, so when button a is pressed the servo will turn 90 degrees, wait 5 seconds and rotate back to 0 degrees. The speaker is connected to the PIN 8 so when the micro bit is shaken it plays a sound an that sound can be turned off by pressing buttons A+B.

Door and light system

This is what the system looks like as a whole. On the exterior is a mircobit that connects to a servo on the inside so if you press button A it will open the door and close it automatically, on the inside when button A is pressed it will also turn on the light. The light is a simple circuit on the breadboard connected to pin 2.

Alarm/Earthquake system

The alarm/earthquake system will activate if the mircobit is shaken. The system consists of a speaker on the outside and a flashing red LED on the inside to warn anyone inside the house. This is helpful for when someone tries to enter the house with out pressing the microbit to open the door and it will scare them off. It is more effective as an earthquake detector since it will detect the motion and warn people. To turn off the alarm all you have to do is press buttons A+B together.
