AUTOMATIC DOOR ALARM
Want a secure but fast way to enter you house without carrying around a key? We got your back! Trying to enter a house when it's dark outside and you can't find the switch to turn on the light, we made it easy for you, when you press the button to open your house it will automatically open the door and turn on the interior light. But what if someone tries to come in without pressing the key? The door will sound an alarm and flash the light o scare your intruder away!

HOW WAS THIS MADE?

This system was created by code, microbit, and a servo.

Here, is a picture of my circuit. I connected the encoder of the servo to Pin 0, the power wire of the servo to pin 3V, and the ground pin to GND. That's essentially how my servo works. Then, with my neatwire management skills, I taped it towards the wall of my house to prevent loose and messy wires. It then escapes through a minature gap in the attic door, lets the servo sit on the floor of the third floor, and controls that door. It's a very capable design, with many functions.
